Description

        This app shows you the known active COVID-19 cases near you along with the number of deaths and number recovered. The data is supplied by Johns Hopkins University Center for Systems Science and Engineering (JHU CCSE) (https://github.com/CSSEGISandData/COVID-19). Other data sources include https://worldometer/coronavirus/ and https://corona.lmao.ninja/

        The world datasource is updated every ten minutes. Country and state-level data is updated at least once a day usually, around 23:59 (UTC).
